About Prezidox Academy

Est. 2026 | Emposting Nigeria's Next Generation of Scholars

1. Our Mission

At Prezidox Academy, our mission is to make high-quality, exam-focused preparation tools accessible to every Nigerian student. We aim to bridge the gap between secondary school graduation and university admission by providing state-of-the-art Computer-Based Test (CBT) practice systems that mirror the exact environment of top-tier university Post-UTME screening exams.

We believe that preparation, not luck, is the key to admission. By offering curated questions, detailed explanations, and gamified challenges, we transform the stressful screening period into a structured journey toward academic success.

2. Founder Story

Prezidox Academy was founded in 2026 by a team of passionate educators and software engineers who noticed a recurring problem: bright, capable students scoring extremely high marks in the JAMB UTME, only to miss out on admission due to lack of exposure to university-specific Post-UTME Computer Based Tests.

Traditional prep centers were expensive, geographical constraints locked out rural applicants, and existing online materials were outdated, filled with errors, or lacked explanations. Prezidox was built as a solution — a premium, mobile-first, and highly engaging online prep academy designed specifically for candidates targeting the University of Lagos (UNILAG) and Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU).

3. Why Prezidox?

We go beyond traditional practice question banks. Our platform is built on proven educational and technological principles to maximize retention, speed, and confidence:

  • Real Exam Simulations: Practice under identical conditions (40 questions in 30 minutes for UNILAG, or 60 minutes for OAU) with strict timers and no immediate answers.
  • Instant Explanations: Learn as you go. Our Topic Drill mode shows detailed step-by-step solutions immediately after each answer.
  • Gamified Competition: Challenge peers in real-time battles, join weekly Study Halls, climb the academic leaderboard, and maintain your daily study streaks.
  • Data-Driven Progress: Track your weak areas with precise subject and topic mastery levels, showing exactly where you need to focus.

4. Core Values

  • Integrity: We provide real past questions and high-quality, verified original questions. No fabricated statistics.
  • Accessibility: Our platform is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, enabling students to study anywhere, on any smartphone or tablet.
  • Aspiration: We motivate students to unlock their highest potential, celebrating their milestones as they move from "Freshman" to "Legend" rank.
